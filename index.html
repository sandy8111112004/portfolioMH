About Me

Self-motivated inventor with strong technical insights in Solid-State Circuits, Microwave Theory and Techniques, and Radio over Fiber Communication. The works have been recognized by prestigious conferences such as IEEE International Solid-State Circuit Conference (ISSCC)x5, IEEE Optical Fiber Communication Conference (OFC)x1, IEEE Radio Frequency Integrated Circuit (RFIC)x2, IEEE International Microwave Symposium (IMS)x4.
